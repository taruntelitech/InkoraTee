About InkoraTee

InkoraTee Pvt. Ltd. is a modern streetwear clothing manufacturer and supplier, co-founded by Tarun Teli and Harsh Suthar. Based in Udaipur, Rajasthan (India), we specialize in bulk production of premium-quality T-shirts with a strong focus on originality, durability, and timely delivery.

We work with wholesalers, retailers, distributors, and corporate clients who require MOQ-based orders, custom designs, and private labeling solutions. Our mission is simple: to provide businesses with affordable, high-quality apparel that helps them grow their brand and serve their customers better.

Every product is crafted with 100% cotton fabrics, modern fits, and exclusive prints, ensuring bulk buyers receive products that are market-ready, durable, and trend-focused.
