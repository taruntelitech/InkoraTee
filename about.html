About InkoraTee

InkoraTee Pvt. Ltd. is a bold and modern streetwear clothing brand co-founded by Tarun Teli and Harsh Suthar, two passionate individuals united by a shared vision — to empower youth through fashion that speaks. Their combined creativity, business acumen, and deep understanding of new-age culture form the foundation of InkoraTee.

Headquartered in Udaipur, Rajasthan (India), InkoraTee stands on the pillars of originality, authenticity, and expression. The mission is clear: to craft high-quality, statement-driven T-shirts that help individuals express their personality with confidence and pride.

Each design is 100% original — inspired by art, technology, and real stories. From premium fabrics to perfect fits and culturally rooted graphics, every detail is carefully chosen to create pieces that are not just worn but remembered.

At InkoraTee, we don’t just make clothes — we build movements. As a registered private limited company, InkoraTee is committed to long-term brand building, creative innovation, and expanding its global reach, one bold statement at a time.
