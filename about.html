About Us

InkoraTee Pvt. Ltd. is a modern streetwear clothing company founded by Tarun Teli, with a vision to redefine how youth express themselves through fashion.

Headquartered in Udaipur, Rajasthan (India), InkoraTee is built on creativity, authenticity, and bold identity. Our mission is simple: to design high-quality, statement-driven T-shirts that empower individuals to wear their personality with pride.

Every design we release is original, meaningful, and rooted in culture, art, and technology. We source premium fabrics, focus on perfect fits, and collaborate with new-age designers to deliver collections that resonate with today's generation.

As a registered private limited company, InkoraTee is more than just fashion — it’s a growing brand with a long-term vision, a creative soul, and an entrepreneurial edge.

Founder & CEO: Tarun Teli
