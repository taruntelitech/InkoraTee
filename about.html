About InkoraTee

InkoraTee Pvt. Ltd. is a bold and modern streetwear clothing brand, co-founded by Tarun Teli and Harsh Suthar. What started as a shared dream has now become a brand that inspires confidence and self-expression among today’s youth.

Based in Udaipur, Rajasthan (India), InkoraTee is built on the values of originality, authenticity, and creativity. Our mission is simple: to design premium-quality T-shirts that reflect culture, identity, and individuality — helping people wear not just fashion, but their true selves.

Every product is crafted with 100% original designs, inspired by art, heritage, and modern culture. From breathable cotton fabrics to stylish fits and exclusive prints, we ensure every piece is made to last and stand out.

At InkoraTee, we believe we are not just creating clothes — we are creating a movement. With a vision to grow globally, InkoraTee continues to innovate, inspire, and empower people through fashion that speaks louder than words.
