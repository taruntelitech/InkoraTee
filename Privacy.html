Privacy Policy

At InkoraTee Pvt. Ltd., your privacy is our top priority. We are committed to protecting the information you share with us and being transparent about how it is used.

Our website is designed to showcase and offer our products. Any personal details (such as name, phone number, address, or payment information) provided while placing an order will only be used to process and deliver your purchase securely.

By using our website, you agree to this privacy policy. Updates to this policy (if any) will always be posted on this page.
